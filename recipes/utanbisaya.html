Healthy Utan Bisaya



  • 1 cup squash, cubed
  • 2 pieces gabi, peeled, cubed
  • 1 1-inch piece ginger, peeled, sliced
  • 3 small tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 small red onion, quartered
  • 10 pieces green beans (abitsuelas), sliced
  • 6 piecesokra, sliced
  • 1 pieces eggplant, sliced into rounds
  • 2 pieces green finger chilies (siling pang-sigang), sliced
  • 1 medium patola, sliced into rounds
  • 2 pieces of freshly fried fish (tilapia or lapu-lapu)
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon fish sauce (patis)
  • salt, to taste
  • 1/2 cup malunggay leaves
  1. Pour water into pot over medium-high heat. Add squash, gabi and ginger, bring to boil. Simmer for 5 minutes or until tender.
  2. Add onion, tomatoes, green beans and okra. Lower heat and simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Add eggplant, chilies, patola and flaked fish, simmer for 2 minutes.
  4. Season with fish sauce and salt.
  5. Stir in malunggay leaves.
  6. Serve hot.
