A Yummy Pancity Chammy



  • 250 grams fresh pancit miki noodles, soaked
  • 1/4 kilo chicken breast, boiled, shredded or sliced
  • 3 tablespoons oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 medium red onion, chopped
  • 1/4 kilo kasim, sliced
  • 3 to 5 pieces shrimps, cleaned, head removed and shelled
  • 1 medium carrot, julienned
  • 250 grams red bell pepper, julienned
  • 1/4 kilo sitsaro (snow peas), stem ends removed
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • salt, to taste
  • ground pepper, to taste
  1. Soak the miki in water to remvoe the saltiness of the noodles, drain just before you will add in the port.
  2. Boil the chicken in the pot, drain, shred the chicking and set aside.
  3. Heat the oil and sauté the garlic and onion.
  4. Add the pork, sauté until browned.
  5. Add the shrimps and shredded chicken.
  6. Add the carrots, red bell pepper and sitsaro, sauté.
  7. Pour in oyster sauce and soy sauce.
  8. Add the drained miki and mix well.
  9. Adust the taste by adding salt and ground black pepper.
  10. Serve while hot.
